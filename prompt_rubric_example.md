# Prompt & Rubric Example

## Prompt
"Explain photosynthesis to a 5th-grade student."

## Ideal Response Characteristics
- Simple language
- Concrete example
- Step-by-step logic
- Positive, encouraging tone

## Scoring Rubric
| Criterion | Score 1 | Score 5 |
|---|---|---|
Clarity | Technical terms, unclear | Age-appropriate & simple |
Accuracy | Scientific inaccuracies | Correct and complete |
Tone | Flat, robotic | Engaging, friendly |
Structure | Unorganized | Clear steps + example |

## Example Evaluation Notes
Strengths:
- Friendly tone
- Correct explanation

Improvements:
- Could include more everyday examples (e.g., plants + sunshine analogy)
